About the band

纸老虎 The Paper Tigers are Beijing’s “hottest new indie rock band,” and they are taking China by storm. With an explosive and “danceable” sound reminiscent of The Clash and The Cure meets Bloc Party and The Arctic Monkeys, the band’s original new music is the creation of experienced and successful musicians from four continents forged together in Beijing’s booming underground scene.

Hailing from China, the US, Europe and Australia, the band’s four members (including Joseph Catanzaro, former lead singer of Australian punk rockers Unpaid Debt) boast award winning musical experience in their home countries and abroad that includes international touring experience, a swag of big name supports, high rotation radio play, film clips broadcast on national networks and record sales topping 10,000.
